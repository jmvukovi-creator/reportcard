REPORT CARD: College basketball Grading Policies

Grade Type Summary
SOS Grade Strength of (Course) Schedule: based on opponent's and opponent-opponent's grades
Efficiency Grade Measure of efficiency in classroom on offense and defense weighted by SOS
Competitive Grade Results only grade (sorta Pass/Fail measure but with SOS) not taking into account efficiency, quality of work, and power performance grades
Power Grade Measure of teams' margin of victories/losses in opponent tests (games) weighted by SOS. Home classroom advantage worth 3 points.
Quality Grade Quality of work measure in classroom taking into account team's quality wins, bad losses, road performance, and perfomance in conference
